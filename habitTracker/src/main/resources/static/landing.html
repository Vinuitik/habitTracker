How it works

Step 01
Create a habit
Name it, pick a frequency, set a start date. Mark it as "default success" if it's something you do unless you fail.
Step 02
Check in daily
Open Today, tap the ring to mark a habit done. Your streak increments immediately. Missed days break it — that's the point.
Step 03
Review progress
Use the Overview to see the full picture, add KPI data points, and watch your trend lines move in the right direction.
Step 04
Stay consistent
The scheduler runs every night. No app to open. Check in, check out. The streaks do the motivating for you.
